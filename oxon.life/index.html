Insights

Please reach us at Contact Us section, if you cannot find an answer to your question.

We help every individual contribute in saving the environment for a better tomorrow by providing them eco-friendly and sustainable products.

Our Mission is to craft, support and drive a positive change in the world with sustainable & eco-friendly alternatives.

Oxon largely focuses on green economy. We are on a mission of uplifting the image of India as a poor country filled of filth, creating a platform that rewards user on being a good citizen and an upgraded version of maps to help the Indian society.
It provides support to poor, small vendors who are not well connected with the internet,  with a platform and a wide delivery network to earn more, minimizing our differences.

After signing up with our application, every user can locate the public dustbins and other locations through the device's GPS.

For every user-submitted site, the user will get credits by which he can buy plants, vermicompost, local crafts and other eco-friendly products from the app.

Now using the inputs of these users, the nearest public dustbins locations will be shown and notified to other users who are travelling and  not much familiar with the place, when they are in 20m range of a community dustbin. 

It also helps people to buy soil, equipment, seeds, plants, and other related equipment online at the best price, comfortable and easy way. 

This initiative encourages people to be more aware of the environment, helps to sustain their home gardening with ease and uses a true vocal for the local formula that helps marginalized vendors to earn a little more. 

Thanks for registering and creating the account. Once the Oxon application is released on Google Playstore, all the users who have created account will be sent a downloading link of application through their registered email and ₹ 300 and other rewards will credit in their balance.
